About

Automatic

Automatic synthesis of program is crucial to easily create a large-sized benchmark without manual effort. We adopt large language model (LLM) to synthesize benchmark programs automatically.

Reliable

Reliable ground truth of bug benchmark is essential to correctly evaluate bug detectors. We adopt program verification and constrained decoding technique to ensure the correctness of ground truth.

Realistic

Bug benchmark should be realistic and similar to human written code. We adopt LLM to synthesize realistic and diverse bug benchmark.
